Shoulder Workouts

Exercise 1: Upright Rows

Instructions:

1. Begin by placing your feet on top of a resistance band about shoulder width apart and hold the two handle over your thighs.
2. Stand upright, keeping your core tight and shoulders back at all times during the exercise, and pull the handles up to your neck as shown. Your two elbows should be in a higher position than your hands as shown.
3. Hold position for more then one second and slowly lower to starting position.
4. Repeat exercise
