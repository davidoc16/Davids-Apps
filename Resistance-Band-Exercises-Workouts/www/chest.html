Chest Workouts

Exercise 1: Chest Press

Instructions:

1. Attach a resistance band at chest height to a door frame or around a pole.
2. Face away from the band.
3. Slightly lean forward and forcefully do a chest press out in front of your body. Keep your arms over your chest and hold for 2 seconds.
4. Slowly release the band and let the handles release back in beside your chest to the starting position.
5. Repeat exercise
