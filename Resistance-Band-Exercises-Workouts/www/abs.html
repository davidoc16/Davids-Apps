Abdominals and Core Workouts

Exercise 1: Kneeling Crunches

Instructions:

1. Attach a resistance band to the top of a door frame using the door attachment.
2. Kneel down facing the door and at least two feet away.
3. Grab both ends of the resistace band in front of your shoulders with both hands.
4. Keep both of your hands by the side of your head and powerfully contract your abs and crunch down through your body.
5. Rise up to starting position while still having your back slightly bent and hands in the same position, then repeat the exercise.


Exercise 2: Russian Twists

Instructions:

1. Sit on the floor and loop a resistace band around both of your feet.
2. Slightly Lean back while keeping your core tight.
3. While squeezing your obliques and contracting your biceps, rotate to your right side. Touch the resistance band to the ground to your side.
4. Powerfully pull the resistance band up and over to your left side and touch the ground.
5. Continue to alternate both sides.


Exercise 3: High Low Side WoodChop

Instructions:

1. Attach a resistance band to the top of a door frame using the door attachment.
2. Attach both ends of the resistance band onto one handle.
3. Standing with your feet parallel to the door, grab the handle with both of your hands.
4. Make sure that the band is straightened out fully, then pull the band down to the knee that is furthest away from the door. As this exercise is been performed your knees should be slightly bent.
5. Bring the band back into the starting position then repeat, and switch sides.


Exercise 4: Reverse Crunch

Instructions:

1. Attach a resistance band to the bottom of a door frame
2. Lie on your back, bending knees 90 degrees.
3. Wrap the resistance band around both feet and scoot back to create tension.
4. Abs tight and back flat, pull your knees toward your shoulders, while contracting your abdominal muscles.
5. Slowly return to starting position and repeat exercise


Exercise 5: Anti-Rotation Band Walkouts

Instructions:

1. Attach a resistance band to the middle of a door. Take a few steps out and turn so that the bands are at your side.
2. Keep your arms straight at all times and pull the band across until it’s straight out in front of your chest. This is your starting position.
3. Without rotating, brace your core and walk out laterally away from the pivot point.
4. Take a number of steps out to the end of the band’s tension and slowly shuffle back to start. Complete a set and repeat for the opposite side.


Exercise 6: Band Anti Rotations

Instructions:

1. Attach a resistance band to the middle of a door. Take a few steps out and turn so that the bands are at your side.
2. Keep your arms straight and pull the band across until it’s straight out in front of your chest. Make sure you’re far enough away that there’s significant tension across your core and shoulders.
3. Squeeze your core and resist the pull to rotate. Keep your body as straight as possible. Complete a set and repeat for the opposite side.
4. Repeat exercise
