Back Workouts

Exercise 1: Seated Horizontal Rows

Instructions:

1. Begin by sitting on a floor and loop a resistace band around your feet as shown, or alternatively a pole, or the bottom of a doorframe.
2. This exercise should be performed by keeping your core tight and your back straight at all times, and forcefully row the two handles into the side of your torso.
3. Squeeze the contraction for more then one second and slowly release.
4. Repeat exercise
