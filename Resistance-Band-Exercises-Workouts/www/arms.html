Exercise 1: Bicep Curl

Instructions:

1. Begin by standing on top of a resistace band with feet about shoulder width apart and grab each handle with an underhand grip.
2. Curl the band up into your body as shown and squeeze the contraction. Keep your elbows in by your sides.
3. Slowly lower the band back to the bottom starting position.
4. Repeat exercise
