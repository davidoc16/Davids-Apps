Leg Workouts

Exercise 1: Russian Squat

Instructions:

1. Firstly place each foot into the end easy grip loops of the CLX.
2. Then feed each arm through the two center easy grip loops with one seal between.
3. Fold and raise arms to shoulder height and place feet hip width apart.
4. Maintain your arms with elbows bent and hands crossed at shoulder level as you slowly squat until your thighs are parallel to the floor.
5. Without leaning forward slowly return to a standing position maintaining a neutral back and neck alignment.


Exercise 2: Front Squats

Instructions:

1. Place your feet on top of a band and hold the handles with an overhand grip. Bring your hands up to shoulder height.
2. Squat all the way down until your quads are below parallel. GET LOW.
3. Powerfully explode up and return to a standing position.
4. Repeat
